About Me

At work, I care about creating products that improve people's lives. I'm always looking for the next problem to solve, process to improve, or design opportunity to capitalize on. I enjoy working with others and am always looking to learn from those around me.

Outside of work, I care about my experiences and the people around me. You might find me browsing a museum, or getting lost in conversation with friends, or telling you about that really cool thing that I saw the other day. When I have extra time I enjoy attempting (poorly) to produce music, skateboarding, or playing video games. I think a lot about my place in the world and how my decisions affect my peers, and I try to keep that in mind when I wake up in the morning.
