The Brotherhood Sign (Spring 2019)

In celebration of Duke's historic 2018-19 Men's Basketball recruiting class and season, I decided to laser cut a 3-layered wooden replica of the team's "Brotherhood" logo. I enjoyed going to games throughout the season and decided to make this piece to take to games and to hone my skills with graphic design for laser cutting.

Using Adobe Illustrator, I split the logo to separate the main body and shape of the logo from the front and back designs that would be added on later. This left me with three laser-cutting toolpaths as shown above, with red indicating a cut and black indicating engraving. I engraved the main body piece on the front so that when I would have to glue the pieces on I could get the placement just right.
