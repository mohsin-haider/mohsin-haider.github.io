Electric Skateboard

September 2019 - Present

We're creating a bluetooth-enabled, unintrusive motor attachment that allows users to motorize their Penny boards.

Overview

Penny boards are small skateboards that have a 22 inch deck. They're usually used for cruising on flatter surfaces and are light and portable. We're in the process of designing and building an attachment that motorizes a Penny board without sacrificing these features and can be controlled by a remote that everyone always has--their smartphone. The phone connects to the board using Bluetooth and allows the user to cruise long distances without using their foot to push off the ground or having to carry around a remote.

Recent Progress

At the moment we have a working prototype of a motor attachment that fits onto the truck of the Penny board and can be controlled by a Bluetooth app on an Android phone.

With a recent grant from the Innovation Co-Lab, we'll be adding a battery to this setup to make the assembly portable, as well as upgrading our attachment materials. Soon we should have fully working prototype that we can ride!
